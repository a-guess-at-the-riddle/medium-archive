Light’s Own Wind

sun rise light east
 — as we

together
star and dream will
 — sing sleep
 — to seek

[ar]peggio
life note arcing

swim across mirror
symmetry turn’d bitter

star fall dark west
 — as we

together
star and a heart
 — apart
 — yet not

darkness myst
egg infinite
my tear will strike clear
upon black water

evil that is my fear
flee from the volunteer
directing
this nothing
diagonal
abrupt rush in

can our heart swim
by light’s
own wind?
