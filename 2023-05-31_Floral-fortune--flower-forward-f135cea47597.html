its meaning
is breathing in

birds singing,

giving living

trees dancing,
enchanting,

mirror’s kin,
negative,
image within

infinite
space counter
to the “out there”

prism’d blue,
circadian

round the Earth

flower forward

prayer maker,
heart creator

poiesis
a poem of
chosen action

unguarded,
breathe parades
through fiery days

’til the end
by our hand
the muddle
we’re sinking in

this lattice,
dual lapis
of Daedalus

time crystal
