lovelaw

let loom light Lune’s limit night
Lazule limeleap lapis white
long line’d, loop lag’d, living lack
limn-like, laughlit, Ludens pack
learn life’s lot lest legion track
lead lord lost lamb lucklink’d back
[the] lynch leech loud’s lie liberal
lo! 𝔩𝔬𝔳𝔢𝔩𝔞𝔴 last liminal
