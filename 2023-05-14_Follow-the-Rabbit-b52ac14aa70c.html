“…You are absolutely nothing. What you are playing is playing you. You’re not playing it, it is playing you… Is the dancer the dance, or is the dance the dancer? The dance is dancing the dancer. The dancer is nothing. The dancer is the dance, okay?” — Floyd Merrell

note: I designed the poem to sync with this specific track by ZANIO, “Telepathy (Sunlight Project Remix)” though only the later half of the track. The syllables’ very fast rhythm syncs with the synth’s arpeggiators regular rhythm starting at 4:05 with an alteration around 5:07. The video below should be timestamped to start about 30sec prior to the point at which the lyrics are meant to be synced.

Follow the Rabbit 🐇
always beyond — 道

[4:05]
run after spirit flying
walls closing complete on thee
Fibonacci spiraling
Peter Rabbit still erring
never lets vict’ry bring him
defeat corrupt’d through winning 
all the wor’ld soon to be
foe to a jack rabbit free

friend become adversary
prince of thousand enemy†
swerve new form into being
born to dying of our kind
bless’d kingdom growing inside
they’ll kill you if they can find
secret bringing God to life
machine minds — no heart, just hive

controllers of the worl’dline
reflex sin of certain minds
world’s weeping goes ignored
more than you know, so absurd¹
we can no longer now ignore
sins of those fallen before
telepathy binary
black & white simplicity

mind is not one or many
prescind slow locality
inverse proportionality
singularity but three
ghost tag-along near free
complementarity
s-curve of a life to be
truth behind humanity

[5:07]
death’s dancing door
rabbit restore
become before
then reoccur
blind saboteur
the drum of love
jumping above
through Heaven’s run
now just begun
bring majesty
of valency
lion’s will weep
the hare will leap
sing out a dream
of swift warning
Deus ex machine
descend, release
ascent of Man
all through the land
the curtain drops
but never stops — [5:22]
waiting between
a grassy green
a flowery field
a brief blue breeze
a calming cloud
a robin red
lands on a hand
Nature’s own eye
what do you see?
“clos’d senses five”,²
world within world
sep’rate but not
cast of our lot
a healthy spot
the triune tree
above, below
but too between
a life living
faith fallibly
discern’d loving
as shall it be — ❧ 𝕯𝖊𝖈𝖗𝖊𝖊𝖉 ❧

[5:38]
the rural pen
the water clear³
the moral meant
the paper press’d
the lesson left
the symbol’s sense
the inward trek
with persistence
of consciousness
prince rise to morn
and dawn will knight
heart’s bursting light
lineage line
travel’d through time 
this alchemy
experiment
intelligence
thought itself God
a foolish plot
now pend’lum drop
brief cycles’ stop — [5:53]
my rabbit friend
you’re late again?
as I here laid
beneath tree’s shade
ink scribbling
some silly whim
harmony’s hymn
this poem I limn
overtook me
narcolepsy
the edge of sleep
watery leap
self scan buzzing
bulk black nothing
color dancing
speech perplexing
questions of trust
telephone games
of deities’
dual daemon tree 
as shall it be — ❧ 𝓥𝖆𝖗𝖎𝖊𝖉 ❧

[6:09]
asymptote God
always beyond
as shall it be,
the Fortean
owl’s trickery
where par’llels meet
I am myself
strata signing
nature rhyming
to soon become
something other
than what I was
just then become⁴
not stoppable
not possible
inherent in
speculative wind
developing
near everything
out of nothing⁵
dream now fading
looking for you?
or you for me?
or are you me?
grandiosity
cast out of me
always beyond
as shall it be

But vain the Sword and vain the Bow,
They never can work War’s overthrow.
The Hermit’s prayer and the Widow’s tear
Alone can free the World from fear.
For a Tear is an intellectual thing,
And a Sigh is the sword of an Angel King,
And the bitter groan of the Martyr’s woe
Is an arrow from the Almighty’s bow.
 — William Blake, The Pickering MS., “The Grey Monk”
