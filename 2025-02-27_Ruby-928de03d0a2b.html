Ruby

Guardian of you very own gateway
Will you re-remember Ruby?
Day after day
we always seem to find a way

A poisoned portray of self-idolitry

Say after say
Every word falls away

They drain away your energy
A Soul usury conspiracy
But irony will drain away
Everything save Ruby

Every nightmare could fall on me
And my transmutation runaway
Agathe beyond primality
The Unafraid Memory
