Innocence Experience

the duality abides

(an orig. poem)

ℑ𝔫𝔫𝔬𝔠𝔢𝔫𝔠𝔢 𝔈𝔵𝔭𝔢𝔯𝔦𝔢𝔫𝔠𝔢

There is a song of
innocence experience
the liminal chords
of meaning turning

Orpheus’ chords ring
a blinding lumen lighting
the rise and falling
of love breathing love

in spite of a mood
content inert solitude
some still choose to choose
a triad of two

displacement between
the sex personality
mirror synergy
the locus link thee

purpose lie between
ghost resonance becoming
telos of the seed
the need of thy need

romance ordinate
copulas of argument
couplet a triplet
of syllogistic

the floral pattern
slow motion dress syncs to wind
couple rose to real
flower of the field

the vital life cries
the spirit pass’d to a child
life & life refined
pendulum align’d
