𝔠𝔬𝔩𝔬𝔯 𝔶𝔢 𝔡𝔶𝔢𝔡

in ye blood lie soul a'folded
a rhyme of Time, now it color ye dyed
Light and Shadow flicker a'quicker
a quicker flicker one never has eye'd

color’d other double & double
ye circuit hummin', now sound ye Light
run through Night across the channel
Sensus Communis of soul unite

wheel of feelings, song a’folded
emotions spoken, now color ye cry
in ye heart a flame enfolded
the shape imploded, now color ye Life

to “another World but in this one”
Memory’s amnesia of immediate
mapping only ever half the love
to trace one’s own lines
fly out from your eyes
and dive line dove right through
the Vanishing Reverse of You!
