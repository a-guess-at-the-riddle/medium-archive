[0:16]
what do you see
my feline friend?
the world between
beyond the bend?

our paw’d protector
guard from spectre
dreamland prancer
vitality dancer

[0:32]
Book of the Dead
cult of the Sun
the jazz of life’s
embodied run

respect thy pet
the silhouette
ninja knight might
fearsome of fight

[0:48]
worship’d as gods
Ra’s Egypt’s guards
Pyramid hero
friend to Pharaoh

mediate mein
until your time
brothers of nap
or child of lap

[1:04]

hops a trot gait
light wind of night
right beyond sight

beyond the bend?

[1:21]
past Night’s mirror
near’ than nearer
this human friend’s
prayer extends

across the bend
the cat can send
ghost growl to ghost
keeps vigil by close

[1:37]
the path thee take
on twist of snake
spine dance of fate
poise’d perfect pace

he’ll demonstrate
the shift weight trait
that faith might take
to liberate
