die game infinite
click Russian roulette
bet all my regret
third of ergodic

labyrinth dragon kid
lineage thread live
filling all there is

coin flip & I guess
choice alone makes this
purpose or pale death
impossible yet

no name next just met
stranger setting set
unique you beget
perspective I sense

your insult and yet
breathe, meet the moment
lift up your broken
eye, grin, poet 𝚜 𝚎 𝚗 𝚍

next chance that I get
turn up resonace
bond, break, and respect
paradox lens lept

geomagnetic
soul protectorate
warpheart & timewept
dead reckon soul set

sail for the object
space time separate

on my silhouette

sequence one thread
mystical heart led
dance discarnate
chords proportionate

harmony high hymn
inert momentum
melody motion
liturgy amen
