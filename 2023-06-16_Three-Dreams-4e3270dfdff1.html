If art escapes our dreams and becomes reality are we dreaming reality into existence? The NeverEnding Story is about a work of art, its eponymous book, that exists through Bastian’s imagination turning. But the dream-to-reality trope must have itself once been someone’s dream.

“just as Bastian is following Atreyu’s story, ‘others’ are following Bastian’s’”¹

Maybe we’re just dreams dreaming each other’s living-dreams into existence, and the world itself is just a dream-becoming-dreamer, a dreamer-catcher becoming dream-catcher. I should point out that I’m entirely reinterpreting the term dreamcatcher to be just a person with no negative or positive connotation regarding the dreams being caught. The three dreams are (1) the dreamer-catcher; (2) the dreamcatcher; (3) the higher-order dream.²

One story?
