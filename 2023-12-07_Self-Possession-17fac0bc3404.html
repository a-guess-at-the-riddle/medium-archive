Self-Possession

the sword & imagined

an orig. poem

“You are what you believe in. You become that which you believe you can become” ― Bhagavad Gita

𝔰𝔢𝔩𝔣-𝔭𝔬𝔰𝔰𝔢𝔰𝔰𝔦𝔬𝔫

if I dance, we dance
if you dance, it’s just math
is the poetmath lifefact
the primrose of the path?

a fire “can’t go back”
a self-blind to fate axe
a Faustpact forgot act?
poised free, now trapped?

if I count, they chant
if you count, it’s just that
is the stoneskip somniway
the devil in the decay?

the stone skips ahead
oscillates, breaks & spreads
period doubling a ladder
incarnating new matter

harmony will wed
“I Eureka!” then dead
a last prayer — new life
comma too small a sight

excess of new hopes
displace all that is gross
nonlinear flood in sin
no win is an end

the repel of one’s growth
pushing on their oath
under veil of the light
dark lies play as polite?

the attract of our lack
pulling on the pack
underneath a rose masque
just Cosmos void black

Knights of Pendragon,
Lady of Lake fashion’d,
a Merlin prayer in Latin,
the sword & imagined
