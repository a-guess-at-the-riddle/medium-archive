A Fallibilist Lost in a Sea of Valence

“You may not think that have you have deep power, but you do. Every single person who realizes unity within their heart, who opens their heart to others, who tries to replace and extend judgement with discernment, you are guiding us as stewards into fourth density.” †⁰

If “new levels, new devils” then “new orders, new angels”.

From the parasite to the anti-being
From the gift-giver to the self-sacrificer

An ecology of the daemon
— the light, protector spirit 
— the deep dark demon

beyond all paradox
beyond all untold cult
beyond all immortal alien
beyond all infinitary ghosts of nonlocality
beyond all interdimensionality
beyond all superposition of demiurge and deity

grant me the strength to remain free,
to ride the way, 道
the subtle, low-energy pathways,
seeking the will’s Lagrange points; and

to “create a moving heaven”†⁰
to remain a last star, & 𝕣𝕖𝕤𝕥𝕠𝕣𝕚𝕟𝕘 𝕗𝕠𝕣𝕔𝕖
to pierce straight through —

order
chaos
reverse
irreverse
fortune
misfortune
excellence
error
good
evil

to anneal oneself into equanimity & self-determinacy
recurring to wisdom, and towards supertautology

to embody a tolerating principle — “knowledge or certainty?”†¹

to “replace and extend judgement with discernment”†⁰
— “always an angel, never a God”†²

“Beyond all ideas of right and wrong, there is a field. I will be meeting you there.” — Rumi
