“In questions of science, the authority of a thousand is not worth the humble reasoning of a single individual.” ― Galileo Galilei

Poem inspired by these vibes and the “holding on for dear life” line in the lyrics. I abused the rhyming dictionary here but it is a fun form that matches the song’s dramatic emotionality. It is not meant to be synced in any particular way other than the rhythm of that single line.

Sia — Chandelier [Band: Designer Disguise Feat. Alex of Osatia] (Punk Goes Pop Cover)

Call a Bluff

In the eye of my mind
an unstoppable guide
in the heart of my heart
none will fathom its life

I see your fear inside
fetal position’d child
slavery tortured dive
may the human survive

holding on for dear life
you deny wrongs design’d
holding on for dear life
wounded advance in light
let potential ignite
may mercy bring you sight

all uncertainty hide
in your certainty’s pride
my new certainty tied
to uncertainty’s guide

the dual mirror divine’d
a reality fire
throw a wrench in the drive
crash the car, kill the vibe
call a bluff in your lie
stun by honest heart’s try
disguise secrecy hide
collapse under my sign
synthesize reason’s why
pierce the eye with my eye

holding on for dear life
against fate of my rise
aegis angel of knight
protect cry of this plight
height of rage of the shy
infinite is blood’s cry

psychology behind
enemy other side
confused by evil’s mind
its pathology’s kind

you rise up just to die
died beneath up will fly
pray a lesson survive
when the sun final’y shines
ignorance of the blind
free to live out their life
