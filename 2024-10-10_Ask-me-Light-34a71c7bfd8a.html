Ask me Light

circle seed — sundial tree
cycle thee —as you me

unseen be — shadow’d sea
beholding— Time’s sheep dream
flame bright tree — red ruby
Future speak — ghostly greet

backwardly — pass through me
we three meet — blind memory
eyes drink scene — on Light’s tea
bittersweet — its heartbeat

this grass seat — where I sleep
shepherding — shade’s edge ring
lightbeat meet — leaf’s secret
release free — entrain’d me

to breath deep — in Life’s lee
bravery — up from weak
as I seek — the Being
to stand on — my own feet

embryonic
being beyond logic
the aeonic
wrapped in the incarnate

embryonic
being beyond magic
enigmatic
wrapped in the somatic

The all of it
folded in upon it
the seed heart’d
heart of harmonic
