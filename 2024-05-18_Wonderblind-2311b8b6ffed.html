Wonderblind

[0:00 — 0:30]

[0:30]
more than I
seem to see
blinding me
to the answer

everything — 
before a
question deems
what I’m after

[long break]

[2:11]
everything is sweet
as it’s tragic
anger blends to laughter

carried, I ride free
on a passion
feeling becomes action

harmony in me
its attractor
madness seeking aster

melodies I sing
Love’s born Labyrinths
memories blind to after

everything in me
calls forth after
shadows of the rafter

every fear in me
I will master
crafter seeking shatter

everything sings sweet
forth its answer
Wonder’s blinded asker

every me will be
moment’s master
faster than we answer

everything will be
as it hasn’t
ashes rising valent

everything will be
as it has to
happen
as imagined

nothing that I see
quite exactly
captures
what I’m after
