A Tangent Universe

— a Cartesian meiosis —

“All the world will be your enemy,
Prince with a Thousand Enemies,
and whenever they catch you,
they will kill you.
But first they must catch you,
digger, listener, runner, prince with the swift warning.
Be cunning and full of tricks
and your people shall never be destroyed.”
― Richard Adams, Watership Down
“A storm is coming, Frank says
A storm that will swallow the children
And I will deliver them from the kingdom of pain
I will deliver the children back the their doorsteps
And send the monsters back to the underground
I’ll send them back to a place where no-one else can see them
Except for me
Because I… am Donnie Darko.”
 — Donnie Darko (2001)

Preferred Accompaniment: Peter Gabriel — A Different Drum (2002 Remaster)

[0:04]
The low-intensity,
social-self-conflict,

its passive aggressive
reputation trick.

The mass surveil
of the populace

through thine eyes,
the homunculus.

[0:21]
Pyramid scheme
of fear in place.

Entrain our brains,
in a liar’s arms race.

Fight or flight,
towards complete control.

Whole wide world,
a petty high school.

[0:38]
Attention captured
on a console.

Human proxies
of mind control.

In nonlocal dreams,
choir alters sing

the hivemind’s song
of suffering.

[0:55]
Some events just seem
guided by design.

At freedom’s end,
a Crossing of the Rhine.

A prophecy sign,
in all creative act.

Its occult system of
symbolic tact.

[1:12]
From nature’s state
to the social contract,

we’ll collectively steer
this ship of tax.

We navigate
Charybdis and Scylla

between a Malthusian trap
and a suicide pact.

[1:29]
Will we ever see
beyond the veil?

Our hologram,
its illusory tale?

[chorus]
[1:37]
Oh, Oz wizard,
you Demiurge scourge.

You’re a cosmic eddy,
just a blind detour.

All karmic transgression
is impermanent.

Return thy spirit
as argument.

[1:57]
Hanging on
to the precipice edge.

The Genesis,
to our Exodus.

The destined King’s rise,
a bright eyed advent.

Touched by mystery
of the firmament.

(short pause)

[2:20]
Our species specialized
its natural way

in movement towards
generality.

But now we belong,
to no longer we.

Leviathan rise
out from the sea.

[2:38]
The fall and rise
and the rise and fall.

Dance of Lucifer,
and the Great Father.

[2:45]
—a Cartesian meiosis—

[chorus]
[2:47]
Now die before,
you die a host

and float on past,
as the meta-ghost.

[2:56]
Become a quark,
and bubble up then

as nature’s swerve,
a Clinamen amen.

[3:07]
A Minitour Maze,
an Icarian fall,

a Daedelan plan,
an Ariadnean trawl.

[3:15]
Amplify all mind,
spiritual midwife.

And grow ever strong,
you Tree of Life.

(short pause)

[3:30]
You will be brave,
never afraid.

You will be kind,
never contained.

A fool’s gambit
to pursue anomaly.

The Sphinx’s riddle
after Man’s meaning.

A gardener
of Possibility

yearning freedom
from finality.

Expanding out,
so much faster than

you can shout,
than you can plan,

Just a man.

— version 2.

(from 2:20 onward)

[2:20]
A call to serve
fundament formation.

A moving Heaven
of incarnation.

Deliver us
from persecution

of technologic
mind pollution.

[2:37]
They won’t let
your worldline

stray too far.
The box of all mind.

[2:45]
 — What guides our hand? —

[chorus]
[2:47]
Fractal timewave,
cycling attractor,

shall you align
in a syzygy last chapter?

[2:56]
The metaphysic,
combinatoric map.

The subliminal
allegory’s trap.

[3:06]
A dance of
blatant synchronicity,

the hypnotic chant’s
reverse psychology.

[3:15]
Either coherency’s weight,
breaks right in time

or we fall in line
to the robot-man mind.

(short pause)

[3:30]
So communicate
to me or them,

one on one
versus everyone.

To be sincere,
ever utterly

and entirely,
intrinsically you.

[3:48]
Because the network
connectivity

will dictate
your generosity.

So prescind my friend
but a small world blend.

Temper the spread
of Hive’s eager ascend.

“What you seek is seeking you” — Rumi
